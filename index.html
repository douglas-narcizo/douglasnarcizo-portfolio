I'm a developer living in Florianópolis - Santa Catarina, a beautiful island at the south of Brazil, sometimes called by people as the "Silicon Island", because of its vibrant technology ecosystem.

During all my career, I dedicate myself to identify demands and problems in all instances of processes, and create paths to find and actually reach their solutions.
